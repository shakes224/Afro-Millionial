Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Thabiso Thebe, Afromillionial was born out of a passion for Africa's development and a commitment to making a positive impact. With many years of combined experience in trade, mining, agriculture, and development finance, our team brings expertise and knowledge to drive business success.

Our Values

  1. Africa First: We prioritize African interests and partnerships.
  2. Sustainability: We integrate environmental and social responsibility into our operations.
  3. Innovation: We embrace cutting-edge technologies and best practices.
  4. Integrity: We operate with transparency, ethics, and accountability.
  5. Collaboration: We foster strong relationships with stakeholders and communities.

Our Business Activities

Afromillionial operates across three key sectors:

  1. Trade: Facilitating cross-border commerce and connecting African markets to global opportunities.
  2. Mining: Exploring and extracting natural resources to fuel economic growth.
  3. Agriculture: Cultivating sustainable food systems and empowering local farmers.
  4. Development Finance: Providing access to capital and financial solutions for African businesses.

Our Impact

Through our work, we aim to:

  1. Create jobs and stimulate economic growth.
  2. Improve livelihoods and community well-being.
  3. Promote African entrepreneurship and innovation.
  4. Foster regional cooperation and integration.
